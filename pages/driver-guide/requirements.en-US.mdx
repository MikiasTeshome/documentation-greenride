


## Driver Requirements

To ensure the safety and quality of our service, drivers with Green Ride must meet the following requirements:

- **Age**: Drivers must be at least 21 years old.
- **Driver's License**: A valid driver's license is required.
- **Driving Experience**: Drivers need a minimum of 1 year of driving experience.
- **Vehicle**: Your vehicle must meet our safety and quality standards.
- **Insurance**: Drivers must have valid auto insurance.
- **Background Check**: A background check is mandatory for all drivers.


# Driver Guide

Welcome to the Driver Guide for Green Ride. This guide provides all the information you need to become a driver with Green Ride and start offering rides to passengers.

## How to Register as a Driver

### Step 1: Download the App
1. Go to the [Google Play Store](#) or [Apple App Store](#).
2. Search for "Green Ride Driver" and download the app.

### Step 2: Create an Account
1. Open the app and click on "Sign Up".
2. Enter your personal details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Password
3. Agree to the terms and conditions and click "Register".

### Step 3: Verify Your Phone Number
1. You will receive an SMS with a verification code.
2. Enter the verification code in the app to verify your phone number.

### Step 5: Vehicle Inspection
1. Schedule a vehicle inspection at one of our approved centers.
2. Ensure your vehicle meets all the requirements and passes the inspection.

### Step 6: Complete Background Check
1. Provide consent for a background check.
2. Our team will review your background check results.

### Step 7: Start Driving
1. Once all the steps are completed and approved, you will receive a notification.
2. Open the app and go online to start accepting ride requests.
