## Driver Support

For any assistance or inquiries, Green Ride offers comprehensive support options for drivers:

1. **Contact support through the app**: 
   - If you encounter any issues or have questions while using the Green Ride Driver app, you can easily reach out to our support team directly through the app.
   - Access the support feature within the app to send messages or request assistance from our dedicated support staff.

2. **Access driver resources and FAQs**: 
   - Green Ride provides a range of resources and frequently asked questions (FAQs) to assist drivers in navigating various aspects of the platform.
   - Explore the driver resources section within the app or visit our website to access helpful guides, tutorials, and answers to common queries.

For prompt and efficient assistance, utilize these support options to address any concerns or inquiries you may have as a Green Ride driver.
