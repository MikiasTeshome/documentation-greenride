## Booking a Ride

To book a ride with Green Ride, follow these simple steps:

1. **Open the App**: 
   - Launch the Green Ride app on your device.

2. **Enter Your Destination**:
   - Input your desired destination into the designated field within the app.

3. **Choose Your Ride Option**:
   - Select your preferred ride option from the available choices (Economy, Minivan, Bajaj, etc.).

4. **Confirm Your Pickup Location**:
   - Confirm your current pickup location to ensure accurate service.

5. **Request a Ride**:
   - Once all details are entered and confirmed, proceed to request your ride.

That's it! Your ride request will be sent to nearby drivers, and you'll be notified once a driver accepts your request.
