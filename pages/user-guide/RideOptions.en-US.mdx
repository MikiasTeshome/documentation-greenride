## Ride History

With Green Ride, you can easily manage your ride history. Here are some of the functionalities available:

- **View Past Rides**: 
  - Access a comprehensive list of all your past rides within the app.

- **Download Receipts**: 
  - Download detailed receipts for each of your past rides for your records.

- **Rate Drivers**: 
  - Provide feedback by rating your drivers after each ride, helping us maintain service quality.

Easily access and manage your ride history with these convenient functionalities provided by Green Ride.
