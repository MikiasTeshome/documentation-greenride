## Ride Options

Choose from the following ride options:

- **Economy**: 
  - The Economy option provides a cost-effective ride for individual passengers.

- **Minivan**: 
  - The Minivan option is suitable for larger groups or families, offering spacious seating and ample luggage capacity.

- **Three-wheel Vehicle**: 
  - Opt for the Three-wheel vehicle option for shorter trips or navigating through narrow streets.

Select the ride option that best fits your needs and enjoy your journey with Green Ride!
